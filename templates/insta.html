Instructions for Uploading Instagram Data:

  1. Log into Instagram and go to your profile page.
  2. Select the three bars in the upper right corner.
  3. Select "Your activity".
  4. Select "Download your information".
  5. Select "Download or transfer information".
  6. Select your account.
  7. Select "Some of your information".
  8. Select "Followers and following".
  9. Select "Download to device".
  10. Select date range "All time".
  11. Select Format "JSON".
  12. Select "Create files".
  13. When you get notified that your file is ready, go back to the "Download your information" page and download your zip file.
  14. Upload your zip file to this page.
